Why We Created This Website:

For our computer science final project, we were given the task of creating a website centered around something we are passionate about. We came up with this idea because we both wanted to improve on our cooking skills. We have created a digital cookbook with all of our attempts at different recipes. Our project consists of a multitude of dishes, both sweet and savory, where we provide the recipe, our final product, our rating, and what we think we could've done better. After completing a number of recipes, we can both agree that we have gotten significantly better at both baking and cooking.
